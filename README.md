# Job Market Analysis Report

Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing 200 data-industry job postings (Glassdoor data) to surface salary, skill-demand, and location trends.

**Tools:** Power BI, Excel

## Key Insights
- **Python, Excel, SQL** are the most in-demand skills, appearing in 59.5%, 55.5%, and 52% of postings.
- **Machine Learning Engineer** is the highest-paying role (~$132K avg), while **Data Scientist** is the most common (117 of 200 postings, ~$115K avg).
- **California and Massachusetts** lead in job volume (39 and 24 postings).
- **Biotech & Pharmaceuticals** is the top hiring industry (24 postings).
- Salary difference between Master's and PhD requirements is modest (~$109K vs ~$113K) — role and skills matter more than degree level.

## Files
- `Job_market_analysis.pbix` — Power BI report (2 pages: Dashboard, Salary by Education)
- `Job_market_powerbi_source.xlsx` — cleaned source data and summary tables

## Dashboard Pages
- **Dashboard:** slicers for State/Seniority/Sector, sector contribution, in-demand skills, job volume by role, salary by title (treemap), salary by seniority
- **Salary by Education:** average salary comparison across degree levels
